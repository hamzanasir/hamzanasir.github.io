Gas Test Manifold

The Gas Test Manifold is a steel chamber constructed for the purpose of handling and reacting toxic gasses. It consists of Flow Controllers, Pressure Transducers, Thermocouples and Actuated/Manual Valves all connected to a PLC. The interface on the right communciates with these devices in order to remotely control the behaviour of the system. The operator can:

  • Open valves by clicking on them
  • Set flow rate of flow controllers by hovering over the circular dialog boxes connected to them.
  • See which gasses occupy which lines by the multi-colored pipes using a BFS algorithm
  • Observe and respond to any irregularities in the system

Try it out for yourself!

Reactor

The reactor is a 100L container in which chemicals are reacted at high temperatures and pressures. Due to these extreme conditions it's necessary for chemists to constantly monitor the state of this vessel even when they're not present in the lab. To accomplish this the reactor is installed with a thermistor and a pressure transducer connected with a PLC constantly updating the state of the reactor to the web interface.

Filter

The filter is 250L container integrated with various sensors and actuators. It is arguably one of the most used devices by the chemists and so needs constant monitioring.
